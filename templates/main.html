Sanapati Cyberstorm is the CTF Club at National Cyber and Crypto Polytechnic, dedicated to honing the skills of future cybersecurity experts.

About Us

Sanapati Cyberstorm is a dynamic community established on November 10th, 2024, dedicated to fostering a collaborative environment where cybersecurity enthusiasts come together to challenge themselves through Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions. This community serves as a hub for honing technical skills, tackling gamified security challenges, and engaging with like-minded individuals to stay ahead in the ever-evolving world of cybersecurity. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced practitioner, Sanapati Cyberstorm is where growth, learning, and teamwork thrive.
